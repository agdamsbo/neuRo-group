Chapter 1 Preamble

This book is created and maintained as part of the R-group on neuroscience research at the Neurovascular Research Group at Aarhus University Hospital, Denmark.

In the group we mainly work in R and use REDCap(Harris et al. 2009) (Harris et al. 2019) for data collection. In this book I will include workflow examples on how to work with data from REDCap in R, but also share tips to buiilding a database in REDCap, a task many PhD-student may only experience once, and hence the individual experience is limited.

The book is build using the bookdown package(Xie 2022) using the bookdown-demo.
